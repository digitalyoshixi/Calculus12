Just letting a value equal to a more complex value temporarily
# Example
$$\lim_{x \to 0}\frac{(x+8)^{\frac{1}{3}}-2}{x}$$
let $u = (x+8)^{1/3}$
$u^{3}= x+8$
$$\lim_{u \to 2} \frac{u-2}{u^3-8}$$
$$\lim_{u \to 2}\frac{u-2}{(u-2)(u^2+2u+4)}$$
$$\lim_{u \to 2} \frac{1}{(u^2+2u+4)}$$
$$\frac{1}{(2)^2+2(2)+4}$$
$$\frac{1}{12}$$
